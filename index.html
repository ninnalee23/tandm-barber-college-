About T & M Barber College

We are dedicated to training the next generation of professional barbers with a bold, classic approach and a modern feel. Students learn in a real barbershop-style environment where they can build confidence, professionalism, and strong technical skills.

Our goal is to help students grow not only as barbers, but as professionals who are ready to serve their community with excellence.

Why Enroll With Us?

  • Professional barber training in a real barbershop environment
  • Community-focused, welcoming atmosphere
  • Enrollment comes with a kit to help you get started
  • Guidance on state requirements and licensing
  • Supportive staff and instructors who want to see you succeed
